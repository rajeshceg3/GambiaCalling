Kachikally

Home to a sacred crocodile pool, Kachikally is a place of myth and tranquility. Generations have come to witness the serene reptiles, believed to hold healing powers. The atmosphere is thick with history, a quiet reverence shared between man and beast.

Tanji Reserve

The sky over the Tanji River is a canvas for hundreds of bird species. It's a world-renowned reserve where the air hums with calls and the flutter of wings. From vibrant sunbirds to majestic ospreys, it is a symphony of nature in perpetual motion.

Golden Shores

The coastline of Gambia is a stretch of unspoiled, golden sand meeting the gentle Atlantic. Life here moves at the pace of the tides. Fishing pirogues paint the horizon at dawn, and the setting sun melts into the ocean, leaving a sky of pastel fire.
